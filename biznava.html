Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the BizNava app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Amrit Ghimire (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

Data You Create

The Application stores data that you create within the app, including:

  • Business Information: Your business profile, including business name and currency preferences
  • Bookings: Appointment details, including dates, times, services, customers, and payment information
  • Clients: Customer contact information, booking history, and spending records
  • Services: Your service offerings, pricing, and descriptions
  • Packages: Bundled service packages and pricing
  • Discounts: Discount configurations you create
  • Inquiries: Lead information and inquiry details

How Your Data is Stored

  • All data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData
  • Data is automatically synced across your devices using iCloud (CloudKit)
  • Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest through iCloud's security measures
  • No account registration is required to use the Application

Calendar Access

The Application requests write-only access to your calendar to create events for your bookings. This feature is optional and can be enabled or disabled in the app settings. The Application:

  • Only creates calendar events (does not read existing events)
  • Only accesses calendars you explicitly grant permission to
  • Does not share calendar data with third parties

Subscription Information

If you purchase a BizNava Pro subscription:

  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store
  • We do not have access to your payment information
  • Subscription status is verified through Apple's StoreKit framework
  • No payment data is stored by the Application

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

Data Sharing

  • No third-party sharing: We do not share your data with any third parties
  • iCloud: Your data is synced through Apple's iCloud service, which is covered by Apple's privacy policy
  • Apple Services: Subscription information is processed by Apple through the App Store, subject to Apple's privacy policy

Analytics

The Application does not use third-party analytics services or tracking tools.

What are my opt-out rights?

You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling it. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network.

To disable calendar access:

  • Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Calendars → BizNava
  • Toggle off calendar access

To disable iCloud sync:

  • Go to Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → iCloud Drive
  • Toggle off iCloud Drive (note: this will affect all apps using iCloud)

The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. Security

The Service Provider is concerned about safeguarding the confidentiality of your information. We provide the following security measures:

  • Local Storage: Data is stored securely on your device
  • iCloud Encryption: All data synced through iCloud is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • No Network Transmission: The Application does not transmit data to external servers (except through iCloud, which is managed by Apple)
  • Apple's Security: Subscription and payment data is handled securely by Apple through the App Store

However, please be aware that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Delete individual items: Delete bookings, clients, services, etc. directly within the app
  • Delete all data: Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • iCloud data: To delete data from iCloud, you can disable iCloud Drive for the app or delete the app's data container through iCloud settings
