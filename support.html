How to Use Finrup

Welcome to Finrup! This guide will help you get started with managing your finances.

Navigation Tabs

  • 🏠Dashboard: View a quick summary of your financial health.
  • 💸Transactions: Browse, search, and filter all your transactions. Tap the + button to add a new transaction.
  • 💳Accounts: Manage your accounts and balances. Add or edit accounts as needed.
  • 📁Categories: Organize and view your transactions by custom categories. Switch between expense and income types.
  • 🔍Search: Quickly find transactions or accounts using keywords. Recent searches are saved for easy access.
  • 👤Profiles: Manage multiple user profiles. Switch profiles to track separate financial data.

Tips for Using Finrup

  • Tap different tabs at the bottom to switch between features.
  • Use the search tab to find past transactions instantly.
  • Categories help you see where your money goes—set them up for better insight.
  • Multiple profiles are perfect for family or business tracking.
