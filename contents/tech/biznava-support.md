## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting Started

**Q: How do I get started with BizNava?**  
A: After opening the app, create your business profile with your business name and currency. Then you can start adding services, creating bookings, and managing clients.

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**  
A: No account is required! BizNava uses iCloud to sync your data across your devices. Just make sure you're signed in to iCloud on your iPhone.

**Q: Is my data backed up?**  
A: Yes! BizNava uses iCloud to automatically sync and backup your data. As long as you're signed in to iCloud, your bookings, clients, and services are safely stored in the cloud.

### Features

**Q: How do I sync bookings with my calendar?**  
A: Go to the More tab → Calendar Settings, and enable "Sync Bookings to Calendar". BizNava will create calendar events for all your confirmed bookings. You can choose which calendar to use.

**Q: What's the difference between the free and Pro versions?**  
A: The free version includes:
- Up to 50 bookings
- Up to 10 clients
- Up to 5 services
- Basic features

BizNava Pro unlocks:
- Unlimited bookings, clients, and services
- Calendar sync
- Service packages
- Discounts
- Inquiry tracking
- Family Sharing (up to 5 family members)

**Q: How do I upgrade to Pro?**  
A: Go to the More tab and tap "Upgrade to Pro". You can subscribe to BizNava Pro Annual, which includes Family Sharing.

**Q: Can I share my Pro subscription with family?**  
A: Yes! BizNava Pro supports Family Sharing, allowing up to 5 family members to use the Pro features with a single subscription.

### Bookings & Clients

**Q: How do I create a multi-service booking?**  
A: When creating a booking, tap "Add Service" to add multiple services to a single appointment. This is perfect for bundled services or appointments with multiple treatments.

**Q: Can I track payments for bookings?**  
A: Yes! In the booking details, you can add payments with different payment methods (cash, card, etc.) and track the remaining balance.

**Q: How do I convert an inquiry to a booking?**  
A: Open an inquiry and tap "Convert to Booking". The inquiry details will be transferred to a new booking that you can schedule.

### Services & Packages

**Q: What's the difference between a service and a package?**  
A: A service is a single offering (e.g., "Haircut", "Consultation"). A package bundles multiple services together at a discounted price (e.g., "Haircut + Styling + Treatment").

**Q: How do I apply discounts?**  
A: When creating or editing a booking, tap "Add Discount" and choose between percentage or fixed amount discounts.

### Troubleshooting

**Q: My data isn't syncing across devices**  
A: Make sure you're signed in to iCloud on all devices and that iCloud Drive is enabled. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → iCloud Drive and ensure it's turned on.

**Q: Calendar events aren't being created**  
A: Check that you've granted calendar access to BizNava. Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Calendars → BizNava and ensure it's enabled. Also verify that calendar sync is enabled in the app's Calendar Settings.

**Q: The app crashes or freezes**  
A: Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, restart your device. If problems continue, please contact us with details about what you were doing when the issue occurred.

**Q: I can't restore my Pro subscription**  
A: Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID that purchased the subscription. If you're still having issues, contact Apple Support or reach out to us.

## Tips & Best Practices

### Organizing Your Business

- **Create clear service names**: Use descriptive names that your clients will recognize
- **Set up packages**: Bundle popular service combinations to increase value
- **Track inquiries**: Convert leads to bookings to grow your business
- **Use availability blocks**: Mark time off to prevent double-booking

### Managing Clients

- **Keep client info updated**: Add phone numbers and email addresses for easy contact
- **Review booking history**: Check a client's past bookings to understand their preferences
- **Track spending**: Monitor total client spending to identify your best customers

### Calendar Sync

- **Choose the right calendar**: Select a calendar that you actively use
- **Review synced events**: Check your calendar app to verify events are created correctly
- **Keep sync enabled**: Leave calendar sync on for automatic event creation
